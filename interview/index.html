Interview with

Taylor McAra

How did you become interested in graphic design?

I've always been interested and appreciated art. I discovered the world of graphic design on Instagram, and I loved the idea of mixing art and functionality together.

Where do you look for inspiration for your designs?

The main place I look for inspiration is Instagram. I follow many dedicated graphic design accounts that gives tips and share interesting work, I also follow individual designers whose work I'm interested in.

What is your favourite software to use?

My favorite software to use is probably Photoshop, though most of the time I’ll use it in tandem with Illustrator I love adding texture to my designs, and for that Photoshop is king.

Why is it important to learn about the history of design?

It's important to learn about the history of design, where it came from and where it is now. Also to study the notable designers of history to get Inspiration from.

Which area of graphic design are you most interested in?

I'm still not sure if I’ve found my niche in the graphic design world yet because I find so many different areas of graphic design interesting such as web design, logo design, photo manipulation, poster design, etc.

Would you prefer to freelance or work for a company?

I’d love to work freelance, ideally. But that's a hard feat to accomplish right off the bat. So I’d be more than happy to work for a company till I build my own clientele and have the ability to go freelance.

Prior to this program, what kind of graphic design experience do you have?

Prior to this program I have no credible experience, but I was interested in it for a while. Coming into this program I have a decent understanding of Photoshop and Illustrator.

What time of the day do you prefer to work on your designs?

I am not a morning person.... I think that answers itself.

What makes a successful website?

The most important thing to me that makes a well-designed website, is simplicity and clarity. Functionality should come before the art of it.
